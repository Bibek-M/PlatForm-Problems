You are given two integers a and b. Your task is to find the Least Common Multiple (LCM) of these two numbers. The LCM of two integers is the smallest positive integer that is divisible by both a and b. This concept is widely used in number theory and can help in solving problems involving periodic events, cycles, and more.

#### Example
Input:


6 8  
Output:


24  
Explanation: The smallest number that both 6 and 8 divide without leaving a remainder is 24.

#### More Examples
Input:

15 20  
Output:


60  
Explanation: The smallest number that both 15 and 20 divide exactly is 60.
