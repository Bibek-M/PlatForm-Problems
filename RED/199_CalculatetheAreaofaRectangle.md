In this problem, your task is to write a program that calculates the area of a rectangle. You will be given two integer inputs: the length and breadth of the rectangle. Your program should compute the area using these inputs and output the result.

The area of a rectangle can be determined with the formula:

[ \text{Area} = \text{Length} \times \text{Breadth} ]

Example:
Suppose you are given the following inputs:

Length: 4
Breadth: 5
The area of the rectangle would be calculated as follows:

[ \text{Area} = 4 \times 5 = 20 ]

So, the output will be 20.
